Welcome to saving the lone wolf!

Debate Circle is intended to make debate inclusive for everyone, not just those who go to powerful schools, or those who don't understand how debate works. Debate Circle saves lone wolf debaters the struggles of travelling al one and not understanding circuit debate, and instead transfers it onto one site accessible to all.
